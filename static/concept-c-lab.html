Who Uses DealSim

Built for practitioners.

Not a game. A training tool used by people with real stakes.

◑
Career Coaches

Use DealSim to run clients through their exact upcoming negotiation scenario before the real conversation. The debrief provides structured coaching data.

"The scenario debrief gives me 10 minutes of structured coaching material per session."
◐
HR Professionals

Train hiring managers on negotiation dynamics from both sides of the table. Run the same scenario as candidate and as recruiter to build empathy.

"Running our managers through the competitive-style opponent changed how they handle counteroffers."
◎
Individuals

Practice before a high-stakes conversation. Run the same scenario 5 times on increasing difficulty. Build pattern recognition against specific opponent styles.

"I ran the salary scenario 4 times before my offer call. The actual call felt slow."

Engine Calibration

Help calibrate the engine.

DealSim's opponent behaviors are built from research and practitioner input. When something feels wrong — an opponent who caves too easily, a score that seems off — that's signal we need.

Feedback goes directly into the scenario calibration queue. We publish our scoring methodology — transparency is not optional.
